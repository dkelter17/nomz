### Ingredients

Cabbage Slaw:

0. 2 cups sliced cabbage
0. 1/4 cup mayo
0. 2 Tbsp chipotle in adobo, minced
0. 2 tsp lime juice
0. Salt & pepper

Fish:

0. 2 fillets tilapia
0. 1 cup panko
0. 1 cup flour
0. 1 egg

### Instructions:

1. Combine ingredients in cabbage ingredient list and set aside.
2. Bread tilapia in flour, egg, panko, then fry.
