### Ingredients

- ¼ cup cider vinegar
- 3 T whole grain mustard
- 2 T minced garlic
- 1 T lemon juice
- 2 T lime juice
- ½cup brown sugar
- 6 T olive oil
- 1 ½t salt
- ground black pepper to taste
- 2 lbs chicken, cut

### Directions

1. Mix all ingredients in large bowl.
2. Marinate in fridge for 8 hours or overnight.
3. Grill or bake chicken.
