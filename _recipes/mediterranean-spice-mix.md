## Spice Mix:
- 3 T dried rosemary
- 2 T ground cumin
- 2 T ground coriander
- 1 T dried oregano
- 1/2 T sea salt
- 2 T cinnamon

## Marinade:
- 2 T olive oil
- 2 T lemon juice
- 1/2 t spice mix

## Directions:
For every 1 lb meat, use 2 T of marinade. Marinate for up to 24 hours.
