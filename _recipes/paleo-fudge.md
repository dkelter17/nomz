## Ingredients: 
- 1/2 cup coconut oil
- 1/2 cup cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup smooth almond butter (or peanut butter)
- 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup
- 1/2 t vanilla

## Directions:
1. Melt coconut oil. 
2. Blend all ingredients together.
3. Pour into paper muffin cups.
4. Chill for 30 min or freeze for 10 min.
