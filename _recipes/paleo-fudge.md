## Ingredients:
- ½ cup coconut oil
- ½ cup cocoa powder
- ½ cup smooth almond butter (or peanut butter)
- ¼ cup honey or maple syrup
- ½ t vanilla

## Directions:
1. Melt coconut oil.
2. Blend all ingredients together.
3. Pour into paper muffin cups.
4. Chill for 30 min or freeze for 10 min.
