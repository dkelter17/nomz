## Ingredients

- 3 1/3 cups sifted flour
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp nutmeg
- 1 cup chocolate chip
- 3 cups sugar

- 1 cup oil
- 4 eggs
- ⅔ cup water
- 2 cups canned pumpkin

## Directions

1. Mix dry ingredients together.
2. Add remaining ingredients.
3. Mix until smooth.
4. Grease three 9x5 in loaf pans. Fill each 1/3 full.
5. Bake for 1 hour at 350ºF.
